Pre-Order Process
How it works

NC Store runs on a pre-order model. We produce exactly what's ordered — zero waste, guaranteed production.

🛒
Step 01
Place your order
Browse the collection. Pick items and sizes. Pay via bKash, Nagad, or card through our secure checkout. You get an instant confirmation.
⚙️
Step 02
We produce
Pre-orders close July 14. We aggregate all orders and send exact quantities to production. Takes 2–3 weeks. No overruns, no unsold stock.
📦
Step 03
Delivered to you
Your order ships via Pathao or Steadfast. Inside Dhaka: 3–5 days. Rest of Bangladesh: 5–7 days. You'll get tracking info via email.
✦
Limited Quantities Per Drop
Once the pre-order window closes, production is locked. No restocks of this collection.
🇧🇩
Made in Bangladesh
Local production, quality-checked materials. Community-first from concept to delivery.
🎨
Designed In-House
Every design is made by the NC team. Original work, exclusively ours.
💰
Funds the Community
Every purchase goes back into NC — events, workshops, and student programs.

About Nerd Community
Built by students,
for students.

Founded on November 3, 2015 — the first student-to-student educational platform of its kind in Bangladesh. What started as a Facebook group helping O Level and A Level students navigate past papers and mark schemes has grown into one of the country's largest student communities.

NC is founded
Founded by Jannatul Ferdous Sigma as Bangladesh's first student-to-student educational community. Starts as a Facebook group for O/A Level students — past papers, mark schemes, academic support.

Education USA collaboration
NC co-hosts a major Education USA seminar at EMK Center, Dhaka — 200+ attendees, chief guest Kelly R. Ryan from the American Center. Community reaches 6,500+ members.
